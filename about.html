About REBELLE

REBELLE is a radio-playing disco/dance-pop artist known for her powerhouse vocals, glam-pop flair, and high-energy live shows. Originally from Croatia and now based in Los Angeles, she holds a Bachelor of Music in Vocal Performance and is a dynamic force in the LA music scene — performing both as a solo artist and lead vocalist in several high-profile tribute bands.

Her original songs have aired on major radio stations, and she's written and placed jingles for national airplay. Rebelle has worked with Grammy-nominated musicians and industry veterans, and is a Recording Academy's Grammy U member herself. She recently headlined an international mini-tour for her single Taboo. With three singles out and a debut album in the works, Rebelle delivers bold, empowering anthems that bring disco into the now — and straight onto your playlist.
